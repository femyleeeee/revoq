# Commercial Support

RevoRacer builds low-latency trading infrastructure for early-stage hedge funds and trading teams.

Commercial support is available for:

- Revoq integration into research and live trading systems.
- Benchmark reproduction on customer hardware.
- Linux host tuning for latency-sensitive workloads.
- Custom journal schemas and dispatch paths.
- Private performance reviews and deployment support.

Open source Revoq is intended to make the core engineering visible. Commercial work focuses on applying it safely to real systems, hardware, and operational constraints.

Contact RevoRacer through `revoracer.com`.
